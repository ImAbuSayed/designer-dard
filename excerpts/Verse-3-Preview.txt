Tutte hoye dil naal khada haan main kalla lonely,
Duniya di bheed vich you are my only,
Jaan kaddi jande ne black tere nain sohniye,
Karda main koshish hiding the pain sohniye.
Pith piche loki whatever they talking about,
Sade es rishte te having no doubt,
Udd-de asmaan vich touching the sky,
Aina karda main pyar never saying goodbye.
